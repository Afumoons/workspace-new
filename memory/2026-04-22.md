- In `scheduler/main.py`, semantic duplicate threshold was raised from `nearest_similarity >= 0.88` to `>= 0.94`, and semantic comparison universe now excludes exact structural clones by filtering out records whose `_structural_fingerprint(other) == strat_fp` before semantic comparison.
